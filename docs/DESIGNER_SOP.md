# ApproveFlow Designer SOP
## Standard Operating Procedure ‚Äî Vehicle Wrap Design Team

---

## Why ApproveFlow Exists

ApproveFlow is your **single source of truth** for every design project. It:

- Eliminates email chaos and version confusion
- Increases approval rates through 3D visualization
- Creates an auditable timeline of every decision
- Hands off cleanly to MyShopFlow for customer approval

**Your job is to produce. The customer's job is to approve.**  
ApproveFlow enforces this separation.

---

## The Locked Workflow (Non-Negotiable)

Every project follows this exact sequence:

```
1. Order Received     ‚Üí System captures WooCommerce data
2. Upload 2D Proof    ‚Üí YOU upload your design file
3. Generate 3D Render ‚Üí System creates realistic visualization  
4. Enter Specs        ‚Üí YOU fill production specifications
5. Generate Proof     ‚Üí System builds approval package
6. Send to Customer   ‚Üí Customer sees MyShopFlow approval page
```

**The progress bar tracks YOUR production milestones, not the customer's approval status.**

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Review Order (Zone 2 ‚Äî Source of Truth)

When you open a project:

1. Read the **customer instructions** carefully
2. Review any **uploaded reference files**
3. Note the **vehicle info** (year/make/model)
4. Check **color requirements**

> ‚ö†Ô∏è This section is **READ ONLY**. You cannot edit WooCommerce data.

---

### Step 2: Upload 2D Proof (Zone 4 ‚Äî First Action)

1. Create your 2D design in Illustrator/Photoshop
2. Click **"Upload 2D Proof"**
3. Add version notes describing what you created
4. Submit

> ‚úÖ The stepper moves to "2D Uploaded" when successful.

---

### Step 3: Generate 3D Render

1. Click **"Generate 3D Render"**
2. Wait for the render to complete
3. Review the 3D visualization

> ‚úÖ The stepper moves to "3D Generated" when successful.

---

### Step 4: Enter Production Specifications

Fill in these fields:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Total SQ FT | Yes | Total material needed |
| Wrap Scope | Yes | Full wrap, partial, etc. |
| Vehicle Year | Yes | From order or customer |
| Vehicle Make | Yes | From order or customer |
| Vehicle Model | Yes | From order or customer |

> These specs appear on the customer's approval page.

---

### Step 5: Generate Approval Proof

1. Verify all specs are complete
2. Check validation status shows all requirements met
3. Click **"Generate Approval Proof"**

> ‚úÖ The stepper moves to "Proof Built" when successful.

---

### Step 6: Send to Customer

1. Click **"Email Proof to Customer"**
2. Customer receives link to MyShopFlow
3. Customer can approve or request revisions

> ‚úÖ The stepper moves to "Sent to Customer" when the email is sent.

---

## Handling Revisions

When a customer requests changes:

1. Their message appears in the **Timeline** (Zone 3)
2. Read their feedback carefully
3. Create a new 2D design addressing their notes
4. Upload as a new version (do NOT overwrite)
5. Regenerate 3D if the design changed significantly
6. Regenerate the approval proof
7. Resend to customer

> üìù Every revision is recorded. Nothing is deleted.

## What You Must NOT Do

| ‚ùå DON'T | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Email proofs manually | Breaks audit trail, bypasses MyShopFlow |
| Skip 2D upload | 3D render needs source file |
| Generate proof without specs | Customer sees incomplete information |
| Edit customer instructions | Source of Truth is read-only |
| Delete old versions | Timeline must be auditable |
| Skip 3D generation | Reduces approval rates significantly |
